There will be 10 rounds and each round has two periods.

Each round, you will be randomly and anonymously paired with another participant to play a game.

At the first period, one of you will be selected at random to be a Decider; the other will be a Receiver. At the second period, your roles will be reversed. So, you will be both a Decider and a Receiver in each round.

Deciders are endowed with {{ Constants.endowment_Decider }}; Receivers with {{ Constants.endowment_Receiver }}. As a Decider, you will decide which part of your endowment to transfer to the Receiver. As a Receiver, you will see the transfer that has been made.

At the end of each round, you will be informed about transfers made and your earnings in that round. Earnings in points is given by the product of the final value when Decider (ED) with the final value when Receiver (ER): ER x ED.
